Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-Nagarik?
E-Nagarik is a platform that offers various government services like voting, licenses, and more.
How do I access my citizen details?
You can access your citizen details by logging into your E-Nagarik account and navigating to your profile.
How do I apply for a license?
Apply for a license through the services section. Choose the 'License' option and fill out the required form.
Is my data secure on E-Nagarik?
Yes, all your data is encrypted and protected with high-level security protocols.
Can I request a copy of my citizenship certificate?
Yes, you can request a copy of your citizenship certificate from your profile page.
How do I check the status of my application?
You can check the status of your application in the 'Services' section under 'My Applications'.
How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password'. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
Who can access my data?
Only authorized government officials can access your data for processing your applications.
Can I use E-Nagarik on mobile?
Yes, E-Nagarik is optimized for mobile devices, and you can access it via any mobile browser.
